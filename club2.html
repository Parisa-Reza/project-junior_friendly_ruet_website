RUET IOT Club

The Internet of Things (IoT) is a revolutionary aspect of the Internet since it is thought to be responsible for developing all future generation technologies.The RUET IoT Club will help students to become proficient and research these technologies. Doing this will help Bangladesh meet her aspirations for sustainable development goals and establish herself as a leader in the fourth industrial sector.

RUET Career Club

RUET Career Club is a totally non-political student organization. Its only aim is to work for the career development of RUET students.It works to develop soft skills like presentation, communication and public speaking of the RUET students. RUET Career Club is the most student-oriented club of BUET, aiming to add values to the RUETians' life and build them more competent for this most competitive world. BCC arranges a series of workshops titled 'Life Talk' where students can fix their life goals and set their career accordingly.
