RUET Analytical Programming Lab

RAPL is not a club. Actully it's a voluntary community and you don't need any membership here If you are from CSE background,you have no better option than this because you can develop your problem solving skills,attend sessions and do contest and marathons which will definitely benefit you in the long run.




RUET Cyber community

Like RAPL, RUET Cyber Community does not require any membership.If you are interested in cyber security or ethical hacking this community is for you.




IEEE RUET RAS

IEEE is the world's largest technical professional organization dedicated to advancing technology for the benefit of humanity. IEEE RUET Robotics and Automation Society Student Branch Chapter has started the journey with the intent on barter the knowledge in the field of Automation, Robotics and IOT based systems spread among the students of all kinds.The membership might cost pretty much but definitely worthy.




Astronomy & Science Society of RUET

ASSR will offer you to explore the science specially in Astronomy field. Here you can observe the sky with telescope,write articles about your scientific thoughts, improve your organizing skill by school camping and also enjoy various seminars,podcasts and events like 'Pi day'.




Society of Computer Aided Designers RUET

SCADR is a RUET based society dedicated to pursuing talent in computer-aided design. It has achieved countrywide reputation.Here the main goal is to help fellow RUETian with the basics of engineering designing and elevating designing skills to the next level. SCADR is a platform that gathers the most creative minds and allows them to express their latent talents.
