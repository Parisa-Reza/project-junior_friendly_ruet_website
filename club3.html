Photographic Society of RUET

Photographic Society of RUET was created in 2006. This club was formed in order to deliver the best photographic potential from the students. If you are a photography lover, this platform is for you.




Onuronon

Onuronon - RUET Cultural Club is one of the best places to practice culture. In the midst of the mechanical schedule of the campus, 'Onuronon' is RUET's favorite art platform, the only cultural club. The main vow of the club is to present the various arrangements of the Bengali entity in various forms in the courtyard of RUET.
